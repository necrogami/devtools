# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors to act in good faith: be respectful, assume positive intent, critique ideas rather than people, and don't harass anyone. Keep discussions focused on the project; unrelated arguments belong elsewhere.

The short version: be the kind of person you'd want to collaborate with. Disagreement is fine and expected; personal attacks, harassment, threats, and sustained bad-faith behavior are not.

Violations may result in your contributions being declined and your access to project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) being removed at the maintainer's discretion. Report concerns privately to **a@c4.io**.

This policy applies to all project-managed spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any repository under `github.com/necrogami/devtools`.
